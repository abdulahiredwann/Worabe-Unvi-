The College of Humanities is one of the fastest growing academies in Weabe University since its official establishment in January 2021 with only two departments in undergraduate programs. College of Medicine and Health Science

College of Medicine and Health Sciences (CMHS) was established on 13/01/2021 G.C. During its establishment, there was only a nursing department which had about 48 students, 8 instructors and 1 staff member in the administrative role . Currently, the college has about 7 departments: Nursing, Public health, Medical Laboratory Science, Medicine, Pharmacy, Anesthesia and Midwifery with a total of 347 students (195 males and 152 females) and 57 staff (52 instructors, 2 technical assistants and 3 administrative staff).
